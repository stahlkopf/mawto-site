Atomization of the Web

The next big thing in textual data atomization.

The future of information is not dictated by the constraints of print. Leveraging big data, and algorithims we can isolate pieces of knowledge most relevant to the topic at hand. Content such as blogs, news, and articles online contains much of the same key points rewritten over and over by different publishers

Atomization

Breaking down articles into a list of abstracted and summarized content based on relevance

Atoms

Each Atom is a short relevant data point relevant to the article's topic

Relevant Outlines

Parsing through the fluff and irrelevant data, extracting only the relevant information

Using dozens of Apps for News and Information?

The methods employed by technology to organize information is not benign. The way Mawto currently interprets and organizes news data changes the nature of the news itself. Mawto does not organize the news in long form such as articles.

Pulling data from multiple resources

We conglomerate information from across the web allowing us to push only short form key points (atoms) that are relevant to the topic at hand

Stop using applications providing reusable pieces of information fluffed into articles
